Kahoot! A Super Fan Page!

I decided to choose Kahoot because it is such a useful educational tool. Kahoot is a game-based learning platform that makes it easy to creat, share, and play learning games or trivia quizzes in minutes. It is a company based in Norway, and was made by a team of three people. These people are Morten Versvik, and the co-founders Johan Brand, Jamie Brooker, and Asmund Furuseth. They created it in collaboration with the Norwegian University of Science and Technology.

 

"Kahoot is a great learning resource for group and individual learning." - A quote from Commonsense.org

How was Kahoot developed?

  1. It was an idea of Professor Alf Inge Wang, who worked at the Dept. for Computer Science.
  2. He developed multiple prototypes.
  3. The prototypes were tested in experiments in collaboration with master students.
  4. The first version was released in 2006 and it was named Lecture Quiz 1.0.
    • This was before modern smartphones.
    • The server was implemented in Java and MySQL.
  5. From 2006 to 2011, 4 versions of lecture quiz were developed with changes including:
    • Usability.
    • Newer technology
  6. Lecture Quiz 2.0 was the first prototype where both the teacher and student clients had web interfaces.
  7. Kahoot! In its version closest to today, was released in 2013.
